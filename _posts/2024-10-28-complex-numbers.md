## Standard Form
$$z = x + iy$$

x is real part
y is imaginary part


## Polar Form

![Complex Plane]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/complex_plane.png)

$$cos(\theta) = \frac{x}{r}$$

$$sin(\theta) = \frac{y}{r}$$

Then:

$$x = r \cdot cos(\theta)$$

$$y = r \cdot sin(\theta)$$

$$z = x+iy$$

$$z = r \cdot cos(\theta) + i  \cdot r \cdot sin(\theta)$$

$$z = r ( cos(\theta) + i \cdot sin(\theta) )$$

## CIS Form

$$z = r \cdot cis(\theta)$$

## Exponential Form

$$z = r \cdot e^{i \theta } $$

## Euler's Formula

$$e^{i\theta} = cos( \theta ) + i sin( \theta ) $$
